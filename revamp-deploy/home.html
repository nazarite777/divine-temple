Built Hands Detail Shop from the ground up in 2008 — 16+ years, 5,000+ vehicles, four-state service area. Self-taught developer. Published author. Music producer. Corporation founder.

In August 2025, with no prior coding experience in any of four areas, everything launched simultaneously — Ya Heard Me written, Eden Consciousness Corporation incorporated in Delaware, the self-taught developer journey began (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Firebase, 15,000+ lines of code), and the music vault opened with 11 projects and 100+ tracks. On the Spring Equinox, March 20, 2026 — Day 4 of the I-N-I Year — the corporation formally launched.
