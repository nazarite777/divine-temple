Package Questions

Can I upgrade from a lower tier package?

+

Absolutely! You can upgrade to any higher tier by paying the difference. For example, if you start with Beginner ($27) and want Explorer ($147), you'd pay $120 more. Contact us for upgrade assistance.

What's the difference between packages and memberships?

+

Packages are one-time purchases with included materials and time-limited access. Memberships ($27/month) provide ongoing access. However, Founding Members get lifetime access with their one-time $347 payment.

Are there payment plans available?

+

Yes! For packages $147 and above, we offer 3-month payment plans. Founding Member can be split into 2 payments of $173.50. Contact us to set up a payment plan.

What if I'm not satisfied with my package?

+

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all packages. If you've engaged with the materials and feel they're not right for you, we'll provide a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
