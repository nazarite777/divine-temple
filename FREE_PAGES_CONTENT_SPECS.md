- Welcoming but honest
- Transformational but realistic

- Short sentences (avg 12 words)
- Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
- Lots of white space

- Always clear where they lead

- Specific member counts ("40+ members" not "thousands")

- Transparency (pricing, cancellation, time commitment)
