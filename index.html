The Sacred Symbolism

Every element of our logo carries deep spiritual meaning and intention

The Eden Consciousness Symbol

Our logo features three sacred dots representing the trinity of awakened consciousness: Spirit (Purple - divine authority and higher wisdom), Mind (Gold - truth, illumination, and divine light), and Body (Blue - cosmic flow and earthly vessel).

This trinity reflects the complete integration of our three-fold nature - Mind, Body, and Soul unified in divine harmony. The Spirit dot channels divine authority and connects us to Source consciousness. The Mind dot radiates truth and enlightenment, illuminating the path of awakening. The Body dot flows with cosmic energy, honoring our physical temple as sacred.

When united, these three aspects form Eden Consciousness - the remembering of who we truly are. This is not about becoming something new, but about recognizing the divine perfection that has always existed within us. The logo serves as a visual anchor for this truth: we are spiritual beings having a human experience, forever connected to the infinite love and wisdom of creation.

Each time you see this symbol, let it remind you that all aspects of your being - spiritual, mental, and physical - are perfectly integrated expressions of divine consciousness. You never left Eden. You are always home in the heart of the Divine.
